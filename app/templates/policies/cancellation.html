Cancellation Policy

Understanding our cancellation terms and refund procedures

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

Cancellation Fees

Refund amounts based on cancellation timing

More than 60 days before departure

10% cancellation fee

90% refund

31-60 days before departure

25% cancellation fee

75% refund

15-30 days before departure

50% cancellation fee

50% refund

7-14 days before departure

75% cancellation fee

25% refund

Less than 7 days or no-show

100% cancellation fee

No refund

Important Notes

  • • Cancellation fees are calculated from the tour departure date
  • • All cancellations must be submitted in writing (email or letter)
  • • Refunds are processed in the original currency and payment method
  • • Bank charges and transaction fees are non-refundable
  • • Group bookings may have different cancellation terms

Refund Process

How to cancel your booking and receive your refund

1

Submit Cancellation Request

Contact our customer service team via email or phone with your booking reference

As soon as possible
2

Cancellation Review

Our team reviews your request and calculates applicable fees based on our policy

Within 24 hours
3

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method

5-10 business days
4

Confirmation

You'll receive email confirmation once the refund has been processed

Within 24 hours of processing
