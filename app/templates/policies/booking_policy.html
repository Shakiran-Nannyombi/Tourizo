Booking Policy

Please read our booking terms and conditions carefully before making your reservation.

Last Updated: July 27, 2025

Booking Confirmation

  • All bookings are subject to availability and confirmation
  • A confirmation email will be sent within 24 hours of booking
  • Your booking is not confirmed until you receive our confirmation email
  • We reserve the right to decline any booking at our discretion

Payment Terms

  • Full payment is required at the time of booking for tours under 30 days
  • For tours over 30 days away, a 50% deposit is required with balance due 30 days before departure
  • We accept major credit cards, mobile money (MTN, Airtel), and bank transfers
  • All prices are quoted in USD but can be paid in local currencies
  • Payment confirmation may take 24-48 hours for mobile money transactions

Group Bookings

  • Special rates available for groups of 6 or more travelers
  • Group bookings require a 60% deposit at time of reservation
  • Final payment due 45 days before departure for group tours
  • Custom itineraries available for large groups
  • Dedicated group coordinator assigned for groups of 10+

Booking Timeline

  • Peak season bookings: Reserve 6-12 months in advance
  • Off-season bookings: Reserve 2-4 months in advance
  • Last-minute bookings subject to availability
  • Custom tours require 8-12 weeks planning time
  • Changes to confirmed bookings may incur fees

Travel Requirements

Essential requirements for all travelers

Passport & Visas

  • • Valid passport with at least 6 months remaining validity
  • • Appropriate visas for all countries on your itinerary
  • • Yellow fever vaccination certificate (required for some destinations)
  • • Passport photos may be required for certain permits

Health Requirements

  • • Consult your doctor about required vaccinations 4-6 weeks before travel
  • • Malaria prophylaxis may be recommended for certain areas
  • • Travel with adequate supplies of personal medications
  • • Inform us of any medical conditions that may affect your tour

Age Restrictions

  • • Minimum age for most tours is 12 years unless specified otherwise
  • • Children under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian
  • • Some adventure tours have minimum age requirements of 16 or 18
  • • Senior travelers (65+) may need medical clearance for certain activities

Important Notice

By making a booking with Tourizo, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by our booking policy, cancellation policy, and terms & conditions.
