Our Story

At Tourizo, we believe that the best way to experience Africa's incredible diversity is through authentic, responsible travel that connects you with the heart and soul of this magnificent continent.

Founded in July 2025 by a group of passionate students, Tourizo started as a school project with a big dream - to make African safari experiences accessible to everyone while promoting sustainable tourism.

Though we're still growing and learning, we're committed to working with local communities and creating meaningful travel experiences that showcase the beauty and culture of Africa. Every booking helps us improve and expand our services.
