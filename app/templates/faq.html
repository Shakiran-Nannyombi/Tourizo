Booking & Reservations

How far in advance should I book my safari?
We recommend booking at least 6-12 months in advance, especially for peak season (June-October) and popular destinations like the Serengeti or Masai Mara. This ensures availability and better rates.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations made 60+ days before departure receive a 90% refund. 30-59 days: 50% refund. Less than 30 days: no refund. We recommend travel insurance for unexpected circumstances.
Do you offer group discounts?
Yes! Groups of 8+ people receive special rates. Contact us for custom group itineraries and pricing. We also offer family packages and honeymoon specials.

Travel & Logistics

What vaccinations do I need?
Yellow fever vaccination is required for most African countries. We also recommend hepatitis A, typhoid, and malaria prophylaxis. Consult your doctor 4-6 weeks before travel.
What should I pack for my safari?
Pack neutral-colored clothing (khaki, beige, green), comfortable walking shoes, a hat, sunscreen, binoculars, and a camera. We provide a detailed packing list upon booking.
Is it safe to travel in Africa?
Yes, our destinations are safe for tourists. We work with experienced local guides and stay in secure accommodations. We monitor travel advisories and adjust itineraries if needed.

Safari Experience

What animals will I see?
You'll likely see the Big Five (lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, rhino), plus giraffes, zebras, wildebeest, and many bird species. Wildlife sightings vary by season and location.
What's the best time to see the Great Migration?
The migration occurs year-round, but peak viewing is July-October in Kenya's Masai Mara and December-March in Tanzania's Serengeti. We can recommend the best timing for your preferred experience.
Are children allowed on safaris?
Yes! We offer family-friendly safaris with age-appropriate activities. Children under 5 may have limited game drive options, but we can arrange alternative activities.

Accommodation & Meals

What types of accommodation do you offer?
We offer luxury lodges, tented camps, and budget-friendly options. All accommodations are carefully selected for comfort, safety, and authentic African experience.
Are meals included?
Yes, all our safari packages include three meals per day. We accommodate dietary restrictions with advance notice. Most lodges offer both local and international cuisine.
